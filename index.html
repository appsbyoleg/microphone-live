How do I use my iPhone as a Bluetooth microphone?

Download Microphone Live + Rec, connect your Bluetooth speaker in iOS Settings, open the app and tap the mic button. Your iPhone instantly becomes a wireless microphone. No cables or extra hardware needed.

What is the best app to use iPhone as a microphone?

Microphone Live + Rec is the best iPhone microphone app in 2026, trusted by 300,000 users with a 4.8 App Store rating. It's the only app that streams to Bluetooth speakers AND records simultaneously, with professional voice presets and karaoke mode.

Does it work with JBL, Bose, and AirPods?

Yes. The app works with any Bluetooth audio device — JBL speakers, Bose speakers, AirPods, Sony headphones, gaming headsets, and any other Bluetooth speaker or headset that pairs with your iPhone.

Can I use it for karaoke?

Yes, there's a dedicated Karaoke Mode. Play music through your Bluetooth speaker, activate karaoke mode in the app, and your voice comes through the same speaker on top of the music. Great for parties and home karaoke nights.

Can I record while streaming live?

Yes — this is the unique feature introduced in version 3.0. Live + Rec mode lets you stream to your speaker and record at the same time. Your recordings are saved to your library and can be renamed, organized, and exported.

Is there a latency issue with Bluetooth?

The app shows real-time latency info so you know exactly what to expect. For presentations and karaoke, the latency is imperceptible. For the lowest possible latency, use wired headphones connected via Lightning or USB-C adapter.
